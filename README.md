# 🛒 E-Commerce Business Intelligence & Operations Analysis

**Python | Data Analytics | End-to-End EDA Project**

---

## 📌 Overview

End-to-end analysis of a large-scale Brazilian e-commerce dataset to uncover insights on **revenue drivers, customer behavior, payment patterns, and operational inefficiencies**.

The project integrates **9 relational datasets** to reconstruct the full order lifecycle and transform raw data into **business intelligence and decision-ready insights**.

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Tech Stack

Python (Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, Seaborn) | EDA | Data Cleaning | Feature Engineering | Data Visualization

---

## 🔄 Process

### 📦 Data Engineering

* Merged 9 relational datasets into a unified analytical structure
* Cleaned missing values, standardized formats, and removed redundant geolocation data
* Translated product categories from Portuguese to English

### ⚙️ Feature Engineering

* Created `item_total` for transaction-level revenue analysis
* Engineered `is_revenue` to classify completed vs non-completed orders
* Aggregated order-level revenue and volume metrics

### 📊 Analysis Performed

* Customer distribution and revenue concentration by region
* Order lifecycle completion and operational bottlenecks
* Product performance (volume vs revenue comparison)
* Payment behavior and installment usage patterns
* Customer satisfaction via review score distribution

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

* **Revenue Concentration:** São Paulo dominates both customer base and revenue, creating regional dependency risk
* **Operational Gaps:** Most order failures are due to cancellations and product unavailability
* **Payment Behavior:** Higher-value orders are strongly linked to installment usage
* **Product Dynamics:** High volume ≠ high revenue; strong imbalance across categories
* **Customer Satisfaction:** ~77% positive reviews indicate strong overall platform experience

---

This project demonstrates how raw e-commerce data can be transformed into **actionable business insights** through structured data analysis, feature engineering, and visualization.

It answers:
